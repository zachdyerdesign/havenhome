OUR SERVICES

Physical Therapy

Offering you evidence based hands on, exercise, and pain science education treatments that are proven to work.

Free Consultations

 

Not sure what physical therapy can do to help you? Schedule a Free Consultation to learn a natural way to heal your body through exercise and pain management.

​

We also offer pre-surgery strength and conditioning for a quicker and less painful recovery. 

Speech Therapy

Offering you speech language pathology services to assist with post-stroke and other common sources of speech disturbance.

Occupational Therapy

Offering you services that work on strength, coordination, and modifications for functional activities throughout the day.

Worker's Compensation

We offer in-house or on-site rehabilitation services for injured workers. We also offer Functional Capacity Evaluations, Post Offer Employment Testing, Job Demands Analysis, and Work Hardening/Work Conditioning.

Sports Injury Rehab

 

Injured on the field? We have your solution. Our clinics are suited for multiple sport athlete's return to field rehabilitation. We can also spend time with coaches and parents for injury prevention services.

Post Surgical Rehab

 

Did you recently undergo surgery? We understand your concerns about recovery after surgery. We are the go to experts for  post surgical care to promote a speedy return to work, school, or sports. 

Fall Risk Evaluations

 

Concerned about your balance or have you fallen recently? Maybe you're parent(s) are in denial that they are at risk of falling. 

​

Let us help you understand your risk for falling and help you create a comprehensive strategy to lower your risk of falling.
